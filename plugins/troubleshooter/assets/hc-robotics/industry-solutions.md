## Troubleshooter Use

Industry context helps explain business impact and expected workflows:

- manufacturing: production-line storage, SAP/WMS integration, accurate material management
- 3PL/e-commerce/express/apparel/retail: high-SKU sorting, returns, labor reduction, order timeliness
- cold chain: harsh environment, reduced on-site labor, sorting accuracy and efficiency

Do not classify a technical fault only from industry. Use industry as impact and workflow context.

## Naming Rules

- Use `OmniFlow` as the official English name for ๆ…งไป“็ฉฟไบ‘็ฎญ.
- Use `product_line` for product family: `omniflow`, `omnisort`, `omnistorage_pro`, `omnistorage_mini`.
- Use `project` or `corpus` for site/project instance: `c134`, future project codes, or customer/site names.
- Keep site-specific IPs, device IDs, layout names, firmware versions, and operational rules in the project corpus, not in company or product-line files.

## Evidence Guardrail

Company and product descriptions explain intended architecture and use cases. They are not proof of field behavior. Field diagnosis still depends on logs, video, configuration, telemetry, and repeated site evidence.

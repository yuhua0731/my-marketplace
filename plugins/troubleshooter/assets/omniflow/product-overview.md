## Positioning

OmniFlow is a large-scale high-speed 3D storage system customized by warehouse height and area.

Official use cases include:

- large logistics warehouse piece picking
- full-box buffering
- semi-finished and finished goods storage in intelligent manufacturing plants
- front warehouses in new retail

## Official Feature Themes

- robot fleet: multiple robot types divide work and operate in parallel
- low operating cost: efficient components, low power consumption, modular maintenance
- convenient installation: low site reconstruction requirement and standard rack installation
- ultra-high space utilization: high-density storage in factories, standard warehouses, and high-bay warehouses
- goods-to-person / tote-to-person: accurate tote handling and flexible point-to-point movement
- flexibility: storage locations and robots can be scaled with business growth
